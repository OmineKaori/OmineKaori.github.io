Introduction

Kaori Omine
Developer Support Engineer

I studied Information and Computer Science at Tokyo University of Science and graduated in March 2021. Then I joined Unity Technologies Japan as a new graduate.
Currently, I mainly provide technical support for Digital Twin customers (automotive, construction, manufacturing, etc.).

I first touched Unity when I was a first-year university student, and I fell in love with the freedom of creating the world I imagined in 3D space. In my free time, I often enjoy making 3D models in Blender or VRoid and importing them into Unity. I also recently started taking painting lessons!
